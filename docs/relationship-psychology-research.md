## 1. Academic/Clinical Foundations / 学术与临床基础

### 1.1 Gottman Institute — 戈特曼研究所

**Sound Relationship House (SRH)** / 声音关系屋
- A seven-level model of relationship health, likened to a house with trust and commitment as walls
- 七层关系健康模型，以信任和承诺为墙壁

**The 7 Principles / 七原则**
1. **Build Love Maps** — 绘制爱情地图：深入了解伴侣的世界、梦想、压力
2. **Share Fondness and Admiration** — 表达喜爱与钦佩
3. **Turn Toward Instead of Away** — 转向而非回避（回应连接请求）
4. **The Positive Perspective** — 积极视角：修复前6年的正面互动
5. **Manage Conflict** — 管理冲突：识别分歧但欣赏一致性
6. **Make Life Dreams Come True** — 实现人生梦想
7. **Create Shared Meaning** — 创造共同意义

**Key Concept: Love Maps** / 爱情地图
- Couples who stay married turned toward each other **86%** of the time
- Divorced couples averaged only **33%**
- Questions: partner's stressors, dreams, fears, favorite soothing methods
- 幸福夫妻转向对方的比例为86%，离婚夫妻仅33%

**Key Concept: Bid for Connection** / 连接请求
- Any attempt for attention, affirmation, affection
- Three responses: Turn Toward, Turn Away, Turn Against
- Missed bids → diminished future bids or seeking connection elsewhere
- 被忽视的请求会减少未来的请求，或导致向他人寻求连接

**The Four Horsemen** / 四骑士（破坏关系的信号）
- Criticism / 批评 — attacking partner's character
- Contempt / 轻蔑 — the greatest predictor of divorce (皱眉、嘲讽)
- Defensiveness / 防御 — victimizing oneself
- Stonewalling / 冷战 — withdrawal to avoid conflict

**Key References:**
- [Gottman Institute](https://www.gottman.com)
- [Sound Relationship House: Build Love Maps](https://www.gottman.com/blog/the-sound-relationship-house-build-love-maps/)
- [Turn Towards Instead of Away](https://www.gottman.com/blog/turn-toward-instead-of-away/)
- Book: *"The Seven Principles for Making Marriage Work"* by John Gottman

### 1.2 Attachment Theory / 依恋理论

**Founders / 创始人**
- **John Bowlby** (1907–90) — 依恋理论的创立者
- **Mary Ainsworth** — 陌生情境实验（Strange Situation Procedure）

**Core Principles / 核心原则**
- Infants need close relationship with primary caregiver for survival and healthy development
- Secure attachments form when caregivers are sensitive and responsive
- 婴儿需要与主要照顾者建立亲密关系以确保生存和健康发展

**Ainsworth's Attachment Patterns / 依恋模式**
1. **Secure Attachment** / 安全型 — caregiver available and responsive
2. **Anxious-Ambivalent** / 焦虑矛盾型 — inconsistent caregiver response
3. **Avoidant** / 回避型 — caregivers emotionally unavailable
4. **Disorganized** / 混乱型 — most insecure, fear-based

**Adult Attachment Styles (Main & Hesse ECR-R)** / 成人依恋风格
- **Secure** — comfortable with intimacy and independence
- **Anxious-Preoccupied** — worried about partner's availability
- **Dismissive-Avoidant** — valuing independence over intimacy
- **Fearful-Avoidant** — afraid of both intimacy and abandonment

**Key Book:**
- *"Attached"* by Amir Levine & Rachel Heller — attachment styles in adult relationships
- Maps adult attachment to dating/relationship behaviors

**Key References:**
- [Attachment Theory — Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Attachment_theory)
- ECR-R: Experiences in Close Relationships — Revised questionnaire

### 1.3 Emotion Focused Therapy (EFT) / 情绪聚焦疗法

**By Sue Johnson** / 苏·约翰逊

**Core Principles / 核心原则**
- Emotions are the key to therapeutic change
- Focus on emotional regulation and interpersonal bonds
- 情绪是治疗改变的关键

**Key Concepts / 关键概念**
- **Emotion schemes** — learned emotional responses from past experiences
- **Primary emotions** vs. **secondary reactions** — identify core feelings beneath defensive reactions
- **Withdrawal and protest** — attachment-related responses to distance
- The "Hold Me Tight" model / 拥抱我吧模型 — 7 conversations for distressed couples

**Techniques / 技术**
- Tracking emotional interactions
- Heightening emotional experience
- Reorganizing emotional responses
- Creating secure attachment bonds

**Key Books:**
- *"Hold Me Tight"* by Sue Johnson
- *"Love Sense"* by Sue Johnson
- *"The Great Guilty Thing"* (various editions)

## 7. Summary / 总结

**Core Insights:**
1. **Gottman's Sound Relationship House** provides the best structured framework for relationship health
2. **Paired app's 5-min daily format + expert partnerships** is the strongest model for engagement
3. **Duolingo's streak/XP/leaderboard system** is the proven gamification template
4. **Attachment theory** provides the diagnostic lens for understanding relationship patterns
5. **EFT** provides the therapeutic intervention framework
6. **Mark Manson's direct, no-BS style** models mass-market accessibility

**Top Priority Features to Implement:**
1. Daily 5-minute relationship exercise
2. Streak tracking for consistency
3. Love Map questions in quiz format
4. Bid recognition exercises
5. Expert-backed content
6. Progress visualization (rings, XP, levels)
7. Both-partner participation requirement
8. Positive psychology focus with actionable feedback
